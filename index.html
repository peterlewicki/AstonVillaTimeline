• 1874 - Club founded by members of the Villa Cross Wesleyan Chapel in Handsworth
  • 1887 - Aston Villa win first FA Cup under Captain Archie Hunter
  • 1880 - Villa win their first trophy, the Birmingham Senior Cup
  • 1897 - Club moves into the Lower Grounds, nicknamed "Villa Park"
  • 1957 - Aston Villa wins FA Cup final against Manchester United, the club's first trophy in over 30 years
  • 1958 - Artificial lights installed at Villa Park
  • 1982 - Aston Villa defeat Bayern Munich in the European Cup final
  • 1992 - Aston Villa become founding members of the FA Premier League
  • 2006 - Long-time chairman Doug Ellis sells club to American businessman Randy Lerner
  • 2016 - Aston Villa ownership changes hands again as Chinese investor Tony Xia buys the team
